Ways to help Ukraine:
  • Put a 💙Ukraine💛 sticker on your laptop, car, phone, window, wherever (just show support!)
  • Watch Ukrainian videos/movies:
    • 20 Days in Mariupol
    • Winter on Fire
    • BYU Making Good - Quilts
    • Video about kidnapped Ukrainian children
  • Listen to Ukrainian Music
  • Study/Learn Ukrainian Language
  • Read about Ukrainian history from Ukrainian figures and works (not just Russian/Soviet history about Ukraine)
    • Red Famine - Anne Applebaum
    • The Gates of Europe: A History of Ukraine - Serhii Plokhy
    • The Russo-Ukrainian War: The Return of History - Serhii Plokhy
  • Vote for US and local candidates that are pro-Ukraine
  • Spread awareness about the war on social media, and in your conversations with others. (Be sure to use reliable sources of information)
  • UnityForUkraine (Instagram) - BYU Organization for Ukraine - Follow and/or Donate
  • RazomForUkraine Resources
  • Donate: Reddit list of vetted charities
    • ↖️Tons of categories depending on where you’d like to focus your aid
  • The best thing you can do is something
